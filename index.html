Positivity

Goal

Oh noo, everyone has been stressed out and in a pretty bad mood. Luckily you have a big heart that's filled with positive energy!
Help cheer everyone up so they can poof back into work and be productive.

Controls

Use wasd keys or arrow keys to move around. Fire positive energy with the space bar.

Hints

  • Move forward as you fire positivity pops. This prevents large crowds of negativity from forming
  • Try moving in a diagnoal direction to increase your range of positivity pops
  • Take a peak at your positivity points for motivation. Wow, look at all the people you cheered up.
